Finding Balance in a Hyper-connected World

Smart guidelines for mindful smartphone use, helping you stay connected while maintaining digital wellness and mental health.

Healthy Daily Screen Time Limits

Category Daily Limit Why This Matters
Social & Chat 30-60 mins Maintain meaningful connections without digital overwhelm
Entertainment 60 mins Balance leisure with other activities
Reading 30-45 mins Engage with content mindfully
Other Apps 15-30 mins Limit non-essential screen time

Total Daily Screen Time: Under 2.5 hours/day

App Categories & Guidelines

Social Networking

Includes: Instagram, Snapchat, Facebook, TikTok, Threads

  • Limit social apps to 30-60 minutes per day
  • Focus on meaningful connections over passive scrolling

Games

Includes: All games, from casual to immersive

  • Use time limits to maintain balance
  • Choose games that encourage social interaction

More Categories

Reading & Reference

Includes: E-books, News apps, Educational content

  • Set dedicated reading times
  • Use Focus Mode to minimize distractions

Health & Fitness

Includes: Fitness trackers, meditation apps, health monitoring

  • Use for tracking and motivation
  • Balance digital tracking with offline activity

Healthy Tips for Digital Balance

Practical strategies to maintain a healthy relationship with your smartphone

Set Time Limits

Use built-in Screen Time controls to set app limits and manage notifications effectively.

Create Focus Modes

Set up custom Focus modes for different activities to minimize distractions.

Phone-Free Time

Designate phone-free periods, especially during meals, sleep, and quality time.

Weekly Screen Time Challenge

Try a Full Sunday Without:

  • Non-essential screen time
  • Social media browsing
  • Gaming apps

Replace With:

  • Outdoor activities
  • Face-to-face interactions
  • Physical books or hobbies
