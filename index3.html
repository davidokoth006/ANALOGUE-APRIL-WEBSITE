FINDING BALANCE

  • Smartphones can boost productivity but also increase stress.
  • Use screen-time insights to see where you might cut back.
  • Intentionally schedule “phone-free” blocks each day.

HEALTHY HABITS

Category Daily Limit Why
Social & Chat 30–60 mins Reduces passive scrolling & comparison.
Entertainment 60 mins max Prevents binge-watching that cuts into sleep.
Gaming 30–45 mins Keeps gaming healthy, avoids isolation.
Reading & News No strict limit Avoid “doomscrolling”—use Focus Mode at night.
Productivity As needed Helps work but watch for procrastination traps.
Health & Fitness No limit Encourages movement and well-being.

HEALTHY TIPS

  • Aim for < 2½ hours/day of non-work screen use: 60 mins before bed, 30 mins after waking, during meals.
  • Pick one screen-free day each week (e.g., Sunday).
  • Use Wind Down Mode 1 hour before bedtime (grayscale + Do Not Disturb).
  • Turn off non-essential push notifications.
  • Set App Timers for time-draining apps in Digital Wellbeing.
  • Engage Focus Mode to block distracting apps during work/study.
